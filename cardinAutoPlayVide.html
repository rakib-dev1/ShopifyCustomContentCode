Step 1

Prepare the device

Fill the Rifinefusion item with serum, turn it upside down, and let it sit for 1–2 minutes to allow the needles to soak up the serum. Make sure to close it securely before use.

Step 2

Clean your face

In the meantime, gently cleanse your face to remove makeup and impurities – this allows your skin to absorb the serum optimally.

Step 3

Start the treatment and use the remaining serum

Start in the center of the face and work your way out by tapping. Repeat this 2–3 times. Gently tap any remaining serum onto your face and neck and let it absorb overnight.

Step 4

Post-treatment care & cleaning of the device

Afterwards, apply a gentle moisturizer – redness usually subsides after 15–20 minutes. Clean the device thoroughly and let it air dry completely.

Step 5

Enjoy the results

Let the treatment absorb and enjoy your radiant, smooth skin. If you have any questions, we are always here for you – we will gladly help you get the best out of your Rifinefusion treatment.
