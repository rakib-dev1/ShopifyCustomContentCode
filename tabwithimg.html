Prepare Treatment Aftercare

Wash and dry your face prior to use, removing all makeup. Make sure your hands are clean. Take the patch and simply peel off the adhesive strips. Avoid touching the adhesive and the darts with your hands.

Apply the patch to the desired area and press gently to ensure it adheres properly. Leave it on for the recommended time.

Remove the patch gently and discard it. Follow up with your regular skincare routine for best results.
